# Empathy Map Canvas

## Flight Booking Application - User Empathy Analysis

---

## USER PERSONA: Frequent Traveler (Sarah, 32, Marketing Manager)

### WHO are we empathizing with?
- **Name:** Sarah Johnson
- **Age:** 32
- **Occupation:** Marketing Manager
- **Background:** Travels frequently for business and leisure (10-15 flights per year)
- **Tech-Savviness:** High - comfortable with online platforms
- **Goals:** Quick, hassle-free bookings with clear information

---

## 1. WHAT do they need to DO?

### Tasks/Actions:
- Search for flights based on destination and dates
- Compare flight options (price, timing, airline)
- Select preferred seats
- Make secure payments
- Receive booking confirmations
- Access booking history
- Modify or cancel bookings if needed
- Check flight status and updates

---

## 2. What do they SEE?

### In their environment:
- Multiple flight booking websites and apps
- Advertisements for travel deals
- Reviews and recommendations from peers
- Social media travel content
- Competitor booking platforms

### In the marketplace:
- Various pricing options
- Mixed user reviews
- Different interface designs
- Promotional offers and discounts
- Loyalty programs

### Pain Points Observed:
- Inconsistent pricing across platforms
- Hidden fees appearing at checkout
- Complicated multi-step processes
- Unclear cancellation policies
- Poor mobile experiences

---

## 3. What do they SAY?

### Public Statements:
- "I just want to book a flight quickly without jumping through hoops"
- "Why do prices keep changing every time I refresh?"
- "The booking process should be straightforward"
- "I need to trust that my payment is secure"
- "Mobile booking should be as easy as desktop"

### Quotes:
- "I've abandoned bookings because the process was too complicated"
- "I want all my travel information in one place"
- "Real-time updates are crucial for my travel planning"
- "I appreciate when sites remember my preferences"

---

## 4. What do they DO?

### Observable Behaviors:
- Compares prices across multiple platforms
- Reads reviews before booking
- Prefers mobile browsing but desktop booking
- Saves favorite routes
- Checks booking status multiple times before travel
- Takes screenshots of confirmations
- Signs up for price alerts

### Actions:
- Opens multiple browser tabs to compare
- Abandons cart if process is slow
- Seeks customer reviews
- Uses credit cards with travel protection
- Checks flight status day before travel

---

## 5. What do they HEAR?

### From Friends/Family:
- "That website has better prices"
- "I had a terrible experience with [platform]"
- "Their customer service is responsive"
- "The mobile app is really user-friendly"

### From Influencers:
- Travel bloggers recommending booking platforms
- YouTube reviews of booking experiences
- Social media tips for finding deals
- Professional recommendations

### From Experts:
- Travel agents suggesting direct bookings
- Financial advisors on secure payment methods
- Tech reviewers on best booking apps

---

## 6. What do they THINK and FEEL?

### PAINS (Frustrations, Fears, Obstacles):
- **Frustrations:**
  - Slow loading times
  - Complex navigation
  - Hidden fees and charges
  - Unexpected errors during payment
  - Poor customer support
  
- **Fears:**
  - Payment security concerns
  - Booking errors or double bookings
  - Inability to modify/cancel bookings
  - Missing important flight updates
  - Data privacy issues

- **Obstacles:**
  - Time constraints for booking
  - Limited payment options
  - Poor mobile experience
  - Lack of clear policies
  - Confusing terms and conditions

### GAINS (Wants, Needs, Measures of Success):
- **Wants:**
  - Fast, intuitive booking process
  - Transparent pricing
  - Multiple payment options
  - Easy modification/cancellation
  - Personalized recommendations

- **Needs:**
  - Secure payment processing
  - Real-time flight information
  - Clear booking confirmations
  - Accessible customer support
  - Mobile-responsive design

- **Success Measures:**
  - Booking completed in < 5 minutes
  - No hidden surprises at checkout
  - Received immediate confirmation
  - Can access booking anytime
  - Smooth modification process

---

## KEY INSIGHTS

### Critical User Needs:
1. **Speed & Simplicity:** Users value quick, straightforward booking processes
2. **Transparency:** Clear pricing and policies build trust
3. **Security:** Payment security is non-negotiable
4. **Accessibility:** Seamless experience across devices
5. **Control:** Easy access to manage bookings

### Design Implications:
1. Minimize steps in booking flow
2. Display all costs upfront
3. Implement robust security measures
4. Ensure responsive design
5. Provide comprehensive booking management
6. Offer real-time updates and notifications

## USER PERSONA: Occasional Traveler (Mike, 45, Teacher)

### WHO are we empathizing with?
- **Name:** Mike Roberts
- **Age:** 45
- **Occupation:** High School Teacher
- **Background:** Travels 2-3 times per year for vacations
- **Tech-Savviness:** Moderate - uses technology but not an early adopter
- **Goals:** Find affordable flights with reliable service

---

### Key Differences:
- More price-sensitive
- Less frequent user - needs intuitive design
- Values customer support heavily
- Prefers desktop for final booking
- Relies heavily on clear instructions

### Specific Needs:
- Step-by-step guidance
- Clear help/FAQ section
- Simple cancellation process
- Email confirmations and reminders
- Responsive customer service
