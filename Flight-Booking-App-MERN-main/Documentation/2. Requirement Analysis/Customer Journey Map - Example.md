## PERSONA: Sarah Johnson - Frequent Business Traveler

**Demographics:**
- Age: 32
- Occupation: Marketing Manager
- Location: Urban area
- Tech Comfort: High
- Travel Frequency: 10-15 times/year

**Goals:**
- Book flights quickly without hassle
- Get best value for money
- Manage bookings easily
- Reliable and secure platform

**Duration: 1-2 days**

Sarah needs to book a flight for an upcoming business trip. She's heard about the flight booking application from a colleague.

#### Customer Actions
- Hears about the platform from colleague
- Searches for the website online
- Reads about features and services
- Compares with other booking platforms

#### Touchpoints
- Word of mouth
- Google search
- Landing page
- About section

#### Thoughts
> "I wonder if this platform is reliable"
> "Does it have the flights I need?"
> "Is it user-friendly?"

#### Emotions

#### Pain Points

- Needs reassurance about security

#### Opportunities
- Clear value proposition on landing page

- Simple, appealing design
- Clear feature highlights

### STAGE 2: CONSIDERATION
**Duration: 30 minutes - 1 hour**

#### Scenario
Sarah visits the website to explore flight options and evaluate if the platform meets her needs.

#### Customer Actions
- Browses the landing page
- Checks available features
- Looks for pricing transparency
- Tries to search for flights without logging in
- Decides to register

#### Touchpoints
- Landing page
- Features section
- Flight search preview
- Registration page

#### Thoughts
> "The interface looks clean and modern"
> "I need to register to see more details"
> "Is my data secure?"
> "Registration seems straightforward"

#### Emotions
ðŸ™‚ Interested, Optimistic, Slightly Cautious

- May be hesitant to share personal information

#### Opportunities
- Guest browsing capability

- Simple registration form
- Social proof (number of users, bookings)

## JOURNEY METRICS & KPIs

### Awareness Stage
- **Website visits:** 1,000/month (target)
- **Bounce rate:** < 40%
- **Time on landing page:** > 1 minute

### Consideration Stage
- **Registration conversion:** > 30%

- **Time to register:** < 2 minutes

### Purchase Stage
- **Booking conversion:** > 60% of logged-in users
- **Average booking time:** < 5 minutes
- **Cart abandonment:** < 20%
- **Booking success rate:** > 95%

### Post-Purchase Stage
- **Booking history views:** 70% of users
- **Support ticket rate:** < 5%
- **User satisfaction:** > 85%

### Loyalty Stage
- **Repeat booking rate:** > 40%
- **Referral rate:** > 15%
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS):** > 50
